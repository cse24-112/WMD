Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department specializes in caring for infants, children, and adolescents. We offer routine check-ups, vaccinations, growth monitoring, and management of common childhood illnesses. Symptoms like high fever, rashes, breathing difficulties, and prolonged vomiting should prompt an immediate pediatric consultation. Our diagnostic services include blood tests, allergy screenings, and developmental assessments.

Geriatrics

Our Geriatrics department provides comprehensive care for older adults, focusing on maintaining independence and quality of life. We screen for common age-related issues such as osteoporosis, dementia, arthritis, and cardiovascular risks. Early signs like memory loss, frequent falls, and unexplained weight loss require a geriatric consultation. Services include cognitive assessments, medication management, and mobility evaluations.

Book an Appointment

Cardiology

The Cardiology department focuses on heart and blood vessel health. We diagnose and treat conditions such as hypertension, heart failure, arrhythmias, and coronary artery disease. Warning signs like chest pain, shortness of breath, or palpitations should not be ignored. Diagnostic services include ECGs, echocardiograms, stress testing, and blood lipid profiles.

Book an Appointment

Neurology

Our Neurology department addresses disorders of the nervous system. Common issues treated include epilepsy, stroke, migraines, and Parkinson's disease. Symptoms like frequent headaches, seizures, memory problems, and weakness should lead to a neurology consultation. We provide diagnostic imaging, nerve conduction studies, and cognitive testing.

Book an Appointment

Physical Therapy

Physical Therapy focuses on restoring movement and function after injuries, surgeries, or chronic conditions. If you experience pain, limited mobility, or post-operative weakness, physical therapy may benefit you. Services include exercise programs, manual therapy, and rehabilitation planning.

Book an Appointment

Radiology

Our Radiology department provides essential imaging services like X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds. These services help diagnose fractures, tumors, organ issues, and more. Signs like unexplained swelling, persistent pain, or injuries may require radiological evaluation. We ensure quick, accurate reporting and diagnosis.
